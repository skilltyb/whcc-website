More than a golf club, Westwood Hills is a place where families gather, friendships deepen, and memories are made year after year.

The Course

Built for every player

Nestled in the rolling terrain of Southwest Poplar Bluff, our 18-hole championship layout is equal parts entertaining and challenging. Don't let the scorecard yardages fool you — Westwood Hills is well protected with strategic bunkering, ample trees, and what many say are the best greens west of the Mississippi. Five par 3s that rank among the toughest holes on the course keep every round honest.

Practice facilities offer a full-length driving range, short game pitching and chipping area up to 50 yards, and a putting green that mimics course conditions daily. Tee times available seven days a week. Members enjoy preferred access and priority booking.

Why Westwood Hills

Not just a club.
A better way to live here.

You don't join a country club for a membership card. You join because Tuesday evenings need a better ending, because your kids need somewhere to go all summer, and because Poplar Bluff doesn't have many places where the whole family can eat, swim, play, and belong. That's what this place is.

⛳
Golf whenever you want

No tee time fees. No per-round charges. Just show up and play 18 — or 9 at sunset, or a quick putting session before work. The course is yours, seven days a week. Members play an average of 65 rounds per year. Try getting that value at a daily-fee course.

🏊
Your kids' summer, solved

Drop the kids at the pool in the morning and pick them up in the afternoon. Swim team, lifeguards, snack bar, friends they'll keep for life. A Full Membership costs less per month than a season at most public pools — and includes everything else the club offers.

🍽️
A real night out — in Poplar Bluff

Hoover's is the best restaurant for 50 miles and it's exclusively for members and their guests (except 3rd Saturday). Filet Oscar, wine pairings, tablecloths — without the two-hour drive. Plus the Westwood Grill for Tuesday burgers when you just want to be easy.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
It's a community, not a facility

The couple you meet at the Member-Guest becomes your Saturday foursome. Your daughter's swim friend's mom becomes your friend. The bartender knows your drink. That's what happens when the same 200 families share a place for years. It compounds.

📅
Something happening every week

Trivia night, couples' golf, kids' movie night, holiday brunches, the Fourth of July blowout, the Club Championship. There's always a reason to come back. People who join expecting to golf end up spending just as much time at the events.

💰
It's more affordable than you think

Most people assume a country club is out of reach. But when you add up the gym membership you're not using, the public pool passes, the green fees at other courses, and eating out twice a week — you're already spending it. You're just not getting all this.

Still on the fence? Come see it for yourself.

Schedule a personal tour and play a complimentary round — no strings attached. If you don't see the value, you'll know. But most people who walk the property end up asking for an application before they leave.

Common Questions

Frequently asked

Is there a waitlist for membership? +
Membership availability varies by category. Contact our membership director to check current availability for your preferred type.
Can I host non-member guests? +
Yes! Golf members receive 2 reduced-rate guest rounds per month. Additional guest visits are available at standard guest rates.
What is the initiation fee? +
Initiation fees vary by membership category and are occasionally waived for promotional periods. Contact our membership office for current schedules.
Are there junior membership options? +
Yes — our Junior Membership is available to members under age 35 at significantly reduced initiation and dues. We also offer Youth Memberships for ages 14–18.
Can I upgrade my membership later? +
Absolutely. Members may upgrade at any time, subject to availability. Downgrades are permitted with 30 days' written notice.
Is the club open year-round? +
The golf course and clubhouse are open year-round, weather permitting. The aquatics complex operates Memorial Day through Labor Day.

Our History

A legacy built
hole by hole

Westwood Hills Country Club was founded in 1936 and has graciously served the Poplar Bluff area ever since. Starting with nine holes carved from the rolling terrain of Southwest Poplar Bluff, the club has grown steadily into the 18-hole championship experience it is today — a picturesque and family-friendly destination for golfers, families, and anyone looking for a true home away from home.

For nearly nine decades, Westwood Hills has been the backdrop for family milestones, business relationships, and sporting triumphs across Butler County and the surrounding region.

1936
The Founding
24 founding members established Westwood Hills on 80 acres west of Poplar Bluff. The original nine-hole course opens to great community fanfare.
1952
Expanding to 18 Holes
The back nine is completed, doubling the course. A new clubhouse with dining room and locker facilities opens alongside the expansion.
1968
Aquatics Complex Opens
The addition of the swimming pool complex transforms Westwood Hills into a true full-service family club.
1985
Course Renovation
A major redesign adds strategic bunkering and reshapes several greens. Course rating climbs to 69.6.
1999
Clubhouse Expansion
Renovation adds The Westwood Grill, expands the main dining room, and creates the outdoor Terrace overlooking the 18th green.
2015
Greens Conversion
All 18 greens converted to premium Mini-Verde Bermuda grass, gaining regional recognition for consistently fast, true putting surfaces.
Today
Nearly 90 Years Strong
Westwood Hills remains the premier private club in Southeast Missouri, serving hundreds of member families with excellence.
A place where families return, friendships deepen, and the game of golf is honored in its finest tradition.

Our Mission

More than a club

Westwood Hills exists to serve our members and the broader Poplar Bluff community. We believe in providing exceptional experiences — on the course, at the table, and in the pool — that bring people together and create memories that last a lifetime.

We are proud to be member-owned and community-rooted, reinvesting in our facilities and programs to ensure Westwood Hills continues to be a place of excellence for another 90 years.
